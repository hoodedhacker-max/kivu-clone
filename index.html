FROM THE VOLCANIC SHORES
OF LAKE KIVU

kivu noir

In the rich volcanic soils that line the
shores of Lake Kivu grow one of the
world’s most exclusive coffee beans, the
Rwandan Red Bourbon Arabica. Kivu noir
is rated amongst the top 1% of the
world’s highest quality single estate
coffees.

TOURS

Kivu noir tours offers a coffee history
lesson, and a nice escape while you enjoy
the magnificent views of the Rwandan
highlands.

Enjoy and explore, with a behind-the-
scenes look into the creation of specialty
coffee.

CAFE

Located in downtown Kigali, Kivu noir
cafe will be your go-to place for a lovely
setting to enjoy a great cup of coffee.

Rated as one of the best coffees in the
world, Kivu noir cafe is a treat for all the
senses.
